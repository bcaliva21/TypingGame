Welcome to Deja Vu!
You wake up in a strange room with no memory of who you are. Luckily, you have a compass in your pocket.

Start

Help your character to survive and navigate around the strange manor. Luckily you have a trusty compass! You can navigate by typing go north, go east, go south, or go west. Read and make sure to pay attention to clues left in bold type. Experiment with typing in simple actions when you see clues in the script. Below are a list of available commands.
